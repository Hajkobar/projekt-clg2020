ReRead

Introduction

Every book is like a little tresure. When we read, we get transferred in spacetime to alternative worlds. But what happens to the books which are no longer read by their owners? Our store offers donated, second-hand books which will certainly satisfy the new owner. By this we also help the enviroment as we lower the need for more new printed books. A book is not for one use only and because of you, it can get a new life and give new readers the same pleasure.
